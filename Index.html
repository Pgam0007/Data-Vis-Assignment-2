The 2022 T20I Cricket World Cup

The 2022 T20 Cricket World Cup marked the 8th iteration of the tournament, which commenced in 2009. Cricket has become a global sensation, with the T20 format gaining the most popularity. This edition of the World Cup took place in Australia, spanning across various stadiums in different states. The tournament witnessed incredible matches and several surprising victories. England emerged victorious, showcasing exceptional performances throughout their campaign.

The chart above illustrates the victories of each team during the tournament, with England leading in the number of wins, closely followed by India and Pakistan. Zimbabwe, on the other hand, managed to secure only one victory, marking the lowest in the tally.

Cricket involves a toss at the beginning of the match, determining which team will bat first. Making the correct choice, considering the pitch conditions, can be decisive in winning or losing. Venues of the Tournament

The tournament spanned across various states in Australia, including Tasmania. Matches were played nationwide, with the Sydney Cricket Ground and The Adelaide Oval hosting the most matches, while the Blundstone Arena in Tasmania hosted the fewest.
